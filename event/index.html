On the first weekend of February 2023, GDC SRM continued their legacy by winning the Global Game Jam, hosted by IMSK at the offices of nextwave multimedia once again. For this edition, two teams participated from SRM under the banner of GDC SRM, out of which the first team comprised Shovit, Shiva, Avinash and Saiyam, who managed to bag the first position in the best gameplay and best art and design.

Congratulations to Abhijeet Rastogi on being selected for the Globalink Mitacs Research Internship Program. Abhijeet is set to be working on investigating the cognitive and neurological effects of spaceflight on NASA Astronauts following their mission in the International Space Station, researching the changes that the brain goes through due to microgravity. He will be working under the guidance of Professor Guiseppe Ilaria at the University of Calgary.
